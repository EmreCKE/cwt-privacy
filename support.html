Custom Workout Timer (CWT)

How to Use

Create custom interval workouts by adding blocks with sets, work time, rest time, and rounds. Add breaks between blocks. Tap START to begin your workout.

Background Audio

CWT plays countdown beeps even when the screen is locked or while playing music on Spotify or YouTube. Make sure your phone volume is turned up.

Saving Presets

After building a workout, tap "Save as Preset" to save it. Load saved workouts anytime from the Preset Trainings screen.

FAQ

No sound?

Make sure your phone is not on silent mode and the volume is turned up. Check the alarm volume setting inside the app.

Timer stops in background?

The timer uses wall-clock time so it always catches up when you return to the app. Beeps may not fire in Expo Go — use the production build for full background support.
